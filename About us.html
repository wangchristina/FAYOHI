About Us

  • Neuroqa was founded by 2 ambitious teens passionate about solving one of the world's biggest problems. We decided to tackle mental illness because over 265 million people worldwide suffer from it. We found that many of today's modern approached to mental ilness don't solve the root of the problem, and they take a "one-size fits all" approach. At Neuroqa, we decided to take a different approach to the problem and find a way to personalize treatments.
