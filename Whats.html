We seek to set up an exemplary model NGO that covers an entire state extensively taking
on-board different age groups with the view of improving the health indices of families and youths in Nigeria

A non-profit organization

Reducing preventable diseases and deaths in Nigeria.

We focus on:


  • Reproductive health and STD
  • Malaria
  • Malnutrition
  • Neglected tropical and non-communicatable diseases
  • Water, santitation, hygene

Our drive is to empower Nigerians by the year 2030 through information, and effective interventions to lead healthier lives irrespective of age, gender, ethnicity and place of residence (rural or urban) through our work.
